Chiropractic


In the following sections I expose a wide variety of information about Chiropractic, its great benefits, results, and the change it exerts on health and well-being. Chiropractic, with more than a century of history, is the most widespread manual alternative medicine therapy in the world. It is regulated by law in more than 70 countries.

The World Health Organization defines Chiropractic as a complementary medicine focused on the diagnosis of the detection, correction and prevention of disorders of the neuromusculoskeletal system, especially of imbalances of the spine that affect the functioning of the nervous system, body and health in general.

Chiropractic is a holistic treatment that can help many people recover their vital energy, internal and external. Some people have severe afflictions that undermine their potential on a day-to-day basis. If this is your case, we can also help you.

There are three phases in the Chiropractic treatment care. The first is to alliviate pain and improve movement. The second is to correct the imbalances that have caused the problem and repair the systems that have been damaged. The third phase is to stabalize and maintain the health and wellness. This phase is essential to maintain the benefits of chiropractic over time. We will make specific adjustments so that the column maintains its optimal position and, therefore, the organism can preserve its innate intelligence.

Common Symptoms that we commonly treat include: Arthrosis and Arthritis, spinal pain, low back pain, neck pain, herniated discs, scoliosis, sciatica, joint pain, fibromialgia, dizziness, vertico, headache, depression, stress, emotional disorders, digestive problems, insominia and tiredness, and injuries, especially from accidents and falls.

Continued Chiropractic care is highly recommended for athletes, pregnant women, children, babies, and elderly people.
